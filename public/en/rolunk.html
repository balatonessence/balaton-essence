About Us

Lake Balaton eventually became more than a destination. It became home.

What we first searched for ourselves — silence, a slower rhythm and a place to recharge — is now something we love sharing with our guests.

It all began with a panoramic little house by Lake Balaton.

Our shared dream came true in 2020, when we found a truly special place in the Balaton Uplands: a timber-beamed home surrounded by quiet, complete with a wine cellar and a view that immediately felt unforgettable.

Originally, we imagined it only for ourselves. A place where we could retreat from the Great Hungarian Plain, slow down and recharge. Then, in October 2021, Bor Mámor Ederics Guesthouse was born.
What had once been only ours opened its doors to others — so we could pass on that same experience.

The story did not stop there. What started as a retreat gradually became a way of life.

Lake Balaton eventually became our home. Year after year, we grew: first with two apartments, then with more, and today we welcome our guests in several directly waterfront apartments on the southern shore.

Since then, we have been continuously shaping this world. We do not only create spaces — we create experiences and connections as well. Many of our guests return again and again, and that is one of the most beautiful forms of recognition for us.

We love creating, planning, developing ideas and improving continuously. Our aim is to shape spaces where guests can feel from the very first moment that they have arrived somewhere truly good.

Bor Mámor Ederics Guesthouse is currently being renewed, so that soon it can welcome guests in an even more beautiful and comfortable form.
